Kernels

• Kernels are great!

‣ kernel-SVM demonstrated empirically success in the last decades

‣ can be used with structured input: images, sequences, ...

• Kernels are slow (in general)!

‣ kernel-based learning typically scales O(n^3) with n-data points

‣ linear kernel machines are much faster to train O(d^3) and test

• Challenge: Learning with big data

‣ good performance and theoretical guarantees

‣ be fast at training / test time
